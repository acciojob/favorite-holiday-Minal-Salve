Ley Ladakh


Ladakh is a region in northern India1234. It is located between the Kunlun mountain range in the north and the main Himalayas to the south1. Ladakh is known for its remote mountain scenery13. It is inhabited by a mix of Indo-Aryan and Tibetan people, who speak an archaic dialect of the Tibetan language1. Leh is the main city in Ladakh3. Ladakh shares borders with Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir, and its neighboring countries are Pakistan, China, and Afghanistan4.
